POM

The Pomeranian (often known as a Pom) is a breed of dog of the Spitz type that is named for the Pomerania region in Germany and Poland in Central Europe.

  • Scientific name: Spitz Pomeranian
  • Pregnancy: 58 - 68 days
  • Sleep Length(Adult): 11 - 14 hours
  • Average Lifespan: 12-16 years

These dogs are cute animals that love to play, love to be stroked and are always happy.
